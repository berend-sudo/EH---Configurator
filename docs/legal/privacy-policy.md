# Privacy Policy — Easy Housing Configurator

*Last updated: 29 June 2026*

## 1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how Easy Building Concepts B.V. ("Easy Housing", "we", "us", "our") handles the personal data you provide when you use our online design configurator (the "Configurator").

Easy Building Concepts B.V. is a company registered in the Netherlands under Chamber of Commerce (KvK) number 78108160, with its registered office at Eikendwarsstraat 10, 6706 BK Wageningen, the Netherlands. We are the controller of the personal data described in this policy.

For any privacy question or request, contact us at info@easyhousing.org.

## 2. What this policy covers

This policy applies to the personal data you give us through the Configurator and to the design overview we generate for you. It does not cover any separate dealings you may have with us later, such as a signed design or build contract, which we will address separately.

## 3. What data we collect

We only collect the data you choose to enter. Through the Configurator we collect:

- your full name;
- your email address;
- your phone number;
- your project location (the place you type in, for example a town and country);
- a Google Maps link to your plot, if you choose to provide one (optional);
- your intended timeline to delivery;
- what the design is for;
- how you heard about us;
- whether you have land and funds available;
- whether you want to receive our newsletter;
- the design you create, including its parameters, the generated reference number, and the design files we produce (the PDF and any DXF file).

We do not collect special categories of data, and we do not ask for them. We do not use analytics or tracking tools, and we do not use marketing cookies. We use only cookies that are strictly necessary for the Configurator to work.

## 4. Why we use your data and our legal basis

We use your data for the following purposes:

- **To generate and send your design.** We create the PDF overview of your design and email it to you. This is necessary to provide what you asked for, and to take steps at your request before any possible agreement.
- **To respond to you and discuss your project.** Our sales team may contact you about the design you created and your possible project. We rely on our legitimate interest in following up on an enquiry you started, and on taking steps at your request.
- **To send our newsletter.** We only do this if you tick the newsletter box. This is based on your consent, and you can withdraw it at any time.
- **To keep records.** We keep a record of designs and enquiries for our own administration and business purposes, based on our legitimate interest in running and improving our business.

## 5. Cookies

The Configurator uses only strictly necessary cookies, which allow the tool to function. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies.

## 6. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with any third party for that party's own marketing.

We use Google Workspace (provided by Google) to send email and to store the design and the details you provide. Google acts as our service provider (processor) for this purpose. Other than this, your data stays within Easy Housing.

We may disclose data if we are required to do so by law, or to establish, exercise, or defend a legal claim.

## 7. Where your data is stored and international transfers

Your data is stored in Google Workspace and within Easy Housing's own systems. Because Easy Building Concepts B.V. is based in the Netherlands and we use Google Workspace, your data is processed and stored in the European Union and may be processed in other countries where Google operates its infrastructure.

If you are in Uganda or Kenya, this means the details you provide are transferred to and stored outside your country. We take this transfer seriously and rely on the safeguards offered by our service provider. Where we transfer your data out of Uganda or Kenya, we do so on the basis of your consent to that transfer and the contractual, organisational, and security safeguards provided by Google as our processor, in line with the Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019 and the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019.

## 8. How long we keep your data

We keep your data for as long as it is needed for the purposes set out above, and for as long as we have a relationship with you. We may keep records of enquiries and designs for our administration after that.

You can ask us to delete your data at any time by emailing info@easyhousing.org. We will do so unless we are required by law to keep it.

## 9. Your rights

Depending on the law that applies to you, you have rights over your personal data. These include the right to:

- access the data we hold about you;
- have inaccurate data corrected;
- have your data deleted;
- restrict or object to how we use your data;
- withdraw consent you have given, such as for the newsletter;
- receive your data in a portable form.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@easyhousing.org. You also have the right to complain to a data protection authority. In the Netherlands this is the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens. If you are in Uganda or Kenya, you may also be able to complain to the data protection authority in your country.

## 10. Being contacted and opting out

When you submit your details, you accept that Easy Housing may contact you about the design you created and your possible project. If you no longer wish to be contacted, email info@easyhousing.org and we will stop. The newsletter is separate and only sent if you opted in, and you can opt out of it the same way.

## 11. Children

The Configurator is intended for people aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact info@easyhousing.org and we will delete it.

## 12. How we protect your data

We use the security measures provided by Google Workspace and apply reasonable organisational measures to protect the data you provide. No system can be guaranteed fully secure, but we take steps to keep your data safe.

## 13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The version that applies is the one published on our website. The date at the top shows when it was last updated.

## 14. Contact

For any question about this policy or about your data, contact us at info@easyhousing.org.
